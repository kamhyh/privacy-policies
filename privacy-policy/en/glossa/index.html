Privacy Policy

Glossa — Vocabulary Learning App

1. Data Collection

Glossa does not collect any personal data. All app data (words, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

2. Local Storage

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Your vocabulary sets and added words
  • Your learning progress and statistics
  • Your preferences (target language, theme, notifications)

3. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. The app works entirely offline.

4. Third-Party Services

Glossa does not integrate any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

5. Security

Since your data is stored locally, it is protected by your Android device's built-in security mechanisms. Uninstalling the app will delete all associated data.

6. User Rights

You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data in Android settings.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: music.music.music.music@gmail.com
